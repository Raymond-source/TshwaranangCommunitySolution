Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Our Vision 2028

To transform the Taxi Industry into a powerful engine for Health and Climate Change Resilience by 2028, ensuring all major Taxi Ranks in Tshwane are hubs for HIV Prevention, Treatment and accessible care, with clean water banking, cooling areas, and empowered workforce.

Our Mission

To provide accessible High quality HIV and Wellness services directly to Taxi Operators, Street Vendors and daily commuters through Fixed Walk-In Health Hubs at major Taxi Ranks: Marabastad, Bosman, Bloedmall, Mabopane Interchange and Donneboom Taxi.

1 Million HIV/TB Campaign

Reaching 1 Million People with HIV Prevention, Treatment & Care

The Goal

To reach 1 Million people across Tshwane with comprehensive HIV and TB prevention, testing, treatment, and care services.

Focus Areas

Targeting Taxi Operators, Street Vendors, and daily commuters at 5 Major Taxi Ranks: Marabastad, Bosman, Bloedmall, Mabopane Interchange, and Donneboom.

Our Approach

Stigma-free Health Education, immediate Linkage to Care, and strategic partnerships to ensure our target groups are medically fit to keep Tshwane growing.
